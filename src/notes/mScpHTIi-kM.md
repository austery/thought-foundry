### 自然界中的囚徒困境：黑斑羚的合作

### Axelrod的计算机锦标赛

**阿克塞尔罗德**邀请了一些世界顶尖的博弈论专家，让他们提交计算机程序进行相互对抗。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">- Axelrod called these programs strategies.</p>
</details>

每个策略都将与所有其他策略以及自身的复制品进行对抗，每场比赛进行200轮。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Each strategy would face off against every other strategy and against a copy of itself and each matchup would go for 200 rounds.</p>
</details>

这一点很重要，我们稍后会再谈。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That's important and we'll come back to it.</p>
</details>

阿克塞尔罗德使用分数代替金币，但收益是相同的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, Axelrod used points instead of coins, but the payoffs were the same.</p>
</details>

锦标赛的目标是尽可能多地赢得积分，最终，整个锦标赛重复进行了五次，以确保成功的稳健性，而非偶然。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The goal of the tournament was to win as many points as possible and in the end, the whole tournament was repeated five times over to ensure the success was robust and not just a fluke.</p>
</details>

阿克塞尔罗德举了一个简单策略的例子：它会在每场游戏开始时合作，并且只有在对手连续两次背叛后才会背叛。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Axelrod gave an example of a simple strategy. It would start each game by cooperating and only defect after its opponent had defected twice in a row.</p>
</details>

阿克塞尔罗德总共收到了14个策略，他还添加了第15个策略，名为“**随机**”（Random），它只是随机地以50%的概率合作或背叛。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

所有策略都被加载到一台计算机上，进行相互对抗。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">All strategies were loaded onto a single computer where they faced off against each other.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">One of the strategies was called Friedman.</p>

<details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Another strategy was called Joss.</p>
</details>

它也以合作开始，但随后它会复制对手上一步的行动。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

然后大约有10%的时间，乔斯会偷偷地背叛。
