### 燃料塑造历史，权力源于掌控

我们的现代世界建立在燃料之上。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Our modern world was built on fuel.</p>
</details>
历史一直由谁发现、谁控制以及谁燃烧那个时代的主导燃料所决定。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">History has been determined by who finds, who controls and who burns the dominant fuel of the age.</p>
</details>
因此，当你戴上“燃料有色眼镜”审视过去时，一切都变得有意义。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So the past makes sense when you look at it wearing fuel-tinted glasses.</p>
</details>
这些不仅仅是今年**费耶音乐节**（Fyre Festival: 一场臭名昭著的豪华音乐节骗局）上非常棒的时尚配饰。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And these are not just a really great fashion accessory for this year’s Fyre Festival.</p>
</details>
事实上，它们是我们领导人塑造历史时必不可少的安全装备。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They are, in fact, essential safety equipment for our leaders, as they seek to shape history.</p>
</details>
例如，在1970年代，由沙特阿拉伯领导的石油卡特尔**OPEC**（Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries: 石油输出国组织）试图切断对美国的石油供应。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>
这立即引发了恐慌，并在数月内导致了经济衰退。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Caused an instant panic, a recession within months.</p>
</details>
当时特朗普27岁，排队等候的汽车蜿蜒数里；普京21岁；习近平20岁。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Trump was 27 when those lines of cars snaked around the block. Putin was 21. Xi was 20.</p>

我们的领导人深知，他们的权力源自燃料。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Our leaders understand, in their bones, their power comes from fuel.</p>
</details>

### 下一次能源转型：从燃料到技术
