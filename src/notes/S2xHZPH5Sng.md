<details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They wanted people to come to YouTube, see videos that interested them, click on at least one of them and watch it for a while.</p>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I mean, if you didn't try to attract attention and get people to click on your links, then you wouldn't really be doing your job.</p>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But there is a second definition.</p>
</details>

我认为更多人认同这个定义，即标题等旨在让读者想要点击超链接的东西，特别是当链接指向价值或兴趣可疑的内容时。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<p class="english-text">And it also talks about teasers that intentionally withhold information to exploit the "curiosity gap."</p>
