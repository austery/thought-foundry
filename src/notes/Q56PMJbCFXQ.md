<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Imagine a chair, and instead of the columns or the supports on each corner of the chair, it's at the midpoint of each side.</p>

### 勒梅苏里耶的创新：V形斜撑与调谐质量阻尼器

当勒梅苏里耶思考这个问题时，他突然灵光一闪。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">As LeMessurier considered the problem, he suddenly had a flash of inspiration.</p>
</details>
他抓起一张餐巾纸，勾勒出一个想法。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">He grabbed a napkin and sketched out an idea.</p>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">These chevrons would transfer the forces to the middle of each face and down to the stilts.</p>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now we have to see the gravity loads, right?</p>

<p class="english-text">The gravity loads are coming down the column.</p>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">When they get to the brace, they need to find their way into the brace.</p>
</details>
好的。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Okay.</p>
</details>
所以你要做的就是移除那里的柱子。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And now they're coming down into the braces.</p>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They get down to the bottom here, and now they continue to go down.</p>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You take that column out, it has nowhere to go except into the brace.</p>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
